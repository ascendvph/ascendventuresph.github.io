About Ascend Ventures

Building local pathways
for global brands

Ascend Ventures is a market entry and strategic partnership firm dedicated to helping global brands find their footing in the Philippines. We understand the nuances — the regulatory landscapes, the consumer behaviours, the distribution networks — that make each market distinct.

From first entry to sustained scale, we serve as the on-the-ground partner that bridges opportunities between international brands and local ecosystems. Our work spans market intelligence, brand localisation, distribution partnerships, and long-term growth strategy.

Based in the Philippines, we operate across the region with a network built on trust, relationships, and results.

Elite Supplements is one of the leading sports nutrition and supplement retailers in Australia, with over 140 stores nationwide and a growing international presence in Singapore and New Zealand.
